Lasagna

Lasagna is a dish made of stacked layers of thin flat pasta alternating with layers of sauce, cheese, and other ingredients. It is a traditional dish of Italian cuisine. The word lasagna is the plural of the Italian lasagna, a diminutive of the word lasso, meaning "little lasagne". The dish is popular in many countries, and is commonly made with a meat-based sauce, but can also be made with vegetables or cheese. It is often served with garlic bread and a green salad.

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6

Ingridients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper flakes
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until evenly brown. Drain off fat, and stir in the tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, oregano, basil, fennel seeds, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Simmer for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, and 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese. Spread 1/3 of the meat sauce in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Top with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, overlapping as needed. Spread with half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat layers, and top with remaining meat sauce. Cover with aluminum foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese. Continue baking for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and hot.
